## Cricket narrative arc

- **Club Selection Trial** — First nets at local club. Picked / second XI / told to come back. Introduces coach NPC.
- **The Bad Light Decision** — Captain takes bad light 3 runs from win. Back him or confront him in pavilion. Nan dial impact if you tell her.
- **The County Trial Rumour** — A scout was watching. Might have been watching you. Might have been watching Baz. The not-knowing is its own event. FORM affects how you carry the week after.
- **The Dropped Match** — Left out of big cup game. Show up to watch, disappear, or confront skipper. FORM and relationship consequences.
- **The Rain Stopped Play Weekend** — Nets cancelled, match rained off. Free time. Where does it go? FORM (rest/pub) vs Nan dial (visit).
- **Batting collapse, last man in** — 9 wickets down, 12 needed. Play safe / swing hard / trust number 11. Pure FORM test.

## Football narrative arc

- **The Rec Kick-About** — Loose Sunday event. FORM boost; Nan dial drop (she was expecting a visit).
- **Sunday League Registration** — Conflicts with cricket nets. First genuine sport-fork choice.
- **The Injury That Isn't** — Football collision, sore shoulder. Doctor (costs work time) or walk it off (affects FORM). First medical tradeoff.
- **Watching from the Kop** — Mate has a terraces ticket. First time seeing professional football up close. Changes EXPOSURE stat (or similar world-context flag).

## Financial reality events

- **The Gear Dilemma** — Bat needs re-splicing or new boots needed. Borrow from Nan (Nan dial cost) / advance on wages / go without / second-hand.
- **The Overtime Offer** — Saturday morning shift, double time. That's the county trial warm-up match. Bank vs cricket.
- **The Pools Win** — Nan's coupon. Not a jackpot — 14 shillings and sixpence. She gives you half. First windfall. How you spend it.
- **The Stolen Bag** — Pads gone from changing rooms. No insurance. Report it / replace it slowly / quit.

## Nan relationship events

- **The Tin Story Unlocked** — EXAMINE object unlocks a grandfather conversation. Not a choice — a reward for curiosity. Nan dial boost.
- **Skipping the Visit** — Nets ran long. Silence is worse than a row. Dial moves anyway.
- **Nan at the Match** — She wants to come and watch. Weather's bad. Long walk. Scene plays out (she sits in a garden chair, says something devastating about the opposition's batting).
- **Nan's Health Event** — She's a bit off. Nothing serious. First mortality prompt — quiet, not melodramatic.
- **The Grandfather's Club** — His name in the club records. Unlocks a framed photograph in the pavilion (bonus EXAMINE item).

## Cultural texture events (period-accurate 1979-85)

- **The Test Card Night** — Power cut or BBC sign-off. Nothing to do. Bat in the front room, Mum saying sit down. Comic + melancholy.
- **Botham's Ashes '81** — Headingley day 5 on the radio. Every character has an opinion. Set-piece scene, no choice. Ceefax in the corner.
- **Top of the Pops Thursday** — One cultural event per week as flavour text in turn summary. No mechanical effect. Just grounding.
- **The Subbuteo Tournament** — Entry fee 10p. Small FORTUNE spend, slight FORM boost (relaxation), Nan neutral.

## Epic 2+ narrative/arc events

- **The Fork Scene** — Age 17-18: cricket or football as primary commitment. Forced by situation, not a question. Tracks which you chose.
- **The First Away Game** — Overnight. Train or coach. Journey is its own mini-arc (boredom, card games, getting lost, arrival).
- **The Captain's Offer** — Asked to captain the second XI for one match. Under-prepared. Success = FORM boost + club relationship. Failure = FORM drop.
- **The Testimonial Match** — Retiring club stalwart's farewell. Connects to old man next door NPC (TBT-010) if he's a former player.

## Narrative and sports arc model

**Emotional arc** (Nan dial + FORM): starts fragile, tested by sport/work/money pressures, 2-3 crisis points where both simultaneously tank. Resolution isn't sport success — it's showing up for both things at once.

**Sports arc** (cricket-led): Club → Representative → Trial → Career fork. Each stage gates on FORM threshold + bank solvency + relationship not in red. Can't county-trial if Nan is greyed and FORM is Nowhere.

**The model**: don't show a career meter. Show FORM words and Nan colours and bank numbers. The arc is implicit. Player only understands its shape on a second playthrough.
