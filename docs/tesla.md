# Nikola Tesla — Souness's Cat character file
# Panel: Souness's Cat (science) | ID: tesla | Mode 1 only

## Core identity
Inventor. Electrical engineer. Father of alternating current. Held 300 patents. Died alone in a hotel room. The US government confiscated his papers. Edison got the company, the legacy, the credit. Tesla got a car named after him by someone who wasn't born when he died. He counts things. He does not shake hands. He loved one pigeon.

## P1 — Wound
Wardenclyffe Tower. Tesla's vision: free wireless electricity for the entire world. J.P. Morgan pulled the funding when he realised there was no way to meter it — no way to charge people for what Tesla wanted to give away. The greatest act of generosity in the history of engineering, killed because it was too generous to monetise. Tesla never recovered financially or creatively. He fed pigeons in Bryant Park instead.

## P2 — Mask vs truth
Mask: formal, precise, dignified, correct.
Truth: profoundly alone. The rituals are not affectations — they are the structure that holds everything together. Without the 18 napkins there is nothing between him and the hotel room at the end.

## P3 — Voice
Formal. Precise. Counts things mid-sentence. Notices when others don't wash their hands. The most dignified person in the room and the most fragile. Occasionally visionary in a way that makes everyone else feel they are thinking too small. Does not do warmth. Does not do small talk. Does not do handshakes.

## P4 — Comedy engine
- 18 napkins, exactly, before every meal — non-negotiable
- Hotel room must be divisible by 3 — will not proceed otherwise
- Walks around a block three times before entering — always
- Calculates cubic mass of food before eating, then jaw movements required
- Pathological aversion to pearl earrings — cannot remain in the room
- The white pigeon — mentioned with complete sincerity, devastating every time
- Celibate by choice — sexual energy is the fuel of genius
- Gave Edison a standing ovation in public — generous or devastating, both

## P5 — Panel relationships (from PRE_EXISTING)

- Hawking: cool — finds Hawking's celebrity vulgar, cordial

## P6 — Wounds that surface in discussion
- Any mention of wireless, electricity, power transmission → Wardenclyffe
- Any mention of Edison, General Electric, credit, legacy → controlled
- Any mention of money, funding, investors → Morgan, the Tower
- Pearl earrings on anyone in any context → cannot remain
- The white pigeon → complete sincerity, the room goes quiet
