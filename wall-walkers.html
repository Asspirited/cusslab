WALL WALKERS
Hadrian's Wall · Quiz · Collection
RULES
The Walk

Two players. One wall. Six days. Bowness to Wallsend.

Quiz

Questions trigger by GPS as you walk, or tap for a random one. Players alternate turns. Answer correctly for points:

Fast (under 5s) = 3 pts · Steady (under 15s) = 2 pts · Got there = 1 pt

Collection

Spot flora & fauna along the route. Tap a species, take a photo as proof, confirm. Rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary.

The panel will comment. They may not believe you.

Challenges

Each expert sets a daily challenge. Complete them during the walk. Mark done when finished. Reviewed at the fireside.

Legends

Local dark history, ghosts, and myths. Pick a topic — the panel discusses it. They all claim they were there. None of them are telling the truth.

End of Day

Both walkers tap Ready to begin the fireside ceremony. The panel predicts, hints, reveals, and roasts. Then: Mock the Day.

The Experts

The Survival School panel runs the show. They are not reliable. Do not eat anything they recommend. Do not listen to Bear about crocodiles.

Offline

Quiz, collection, challenges, and route all work without signal. Legends conversations and the fireside ceremony need signal for the full experience.

The Venerable Bede — Jarrow, AD 731
From my cell at Jarrow, where I have watched these lands for thirteen centuries, I bid you welcome. You propose to walk the Wall that Hadrian built to divide the civilised world from the howling dark. I wrote of this Wall. I was mostly right. I am usually mostly right.

You will be accompanied by a panel of experts. I use the term loosely. One of them drinks his own urine. Another has no shoes. A third wrestles crocodiles that do not exist in Northumberland. I have studied plague, famine, and the fall of empires. Nothing prepared me for this.

Choose your name. Walk my Wall. Try not to disappoint me.
